The Ontario Guzzi Riders

. . . are a loosely-knit bunch of motorcycle enthusiasts who believe that Moto Guzzi motorcycles can provide a satisfaction of ownership and a joy of riding that few other motorcycles can. We tend to be mature riders who realize that spec sheets and race results don't tell the whole story, and we enjoy the company of like-minded enthusiasts, which is why the Ontario Guzzi Riders came into being..
